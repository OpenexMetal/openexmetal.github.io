Openex CNC Machining Service for Tube Sheets

Openex Metal

Feb 17, 2022, 1:07 PM UTC+8

Openex is a professional metal manufacturer, we have over 10 years’ experience in heavy fabrication & large machining, like large tube sheet for industrial reactor & pressure vessel, and heat exchanger baffle plate.
Our tube sheet workshop has 52 sets of machining equipment, such as Multi Spindle Drilling Machines, Deep Hole Drilling Machines, Large Boring & Milling Machining Centers.

  • Specifications of our Large Boring & Milling Center
  • Height × Width: 25×8 m
  • Rotary Table: 9000×5000 mm
  • Spindle Rotation Indexing: 0.001°
  • Maximum Machining Dimensions: 9000×13000 mm
  • Boring Spindle Diameter: 280 mm
  • Maximum Stroke of Ram: 2000 mm

We can handle thick plate machining, with a thickness of 1200 mm, and up to 8000 mm diameter, as well as simultaneous machining for tube sheets.
By shortening the machining time, the manufacturing efficiency is greatly improved.
