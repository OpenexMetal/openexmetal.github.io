What product does Openex manufacture?

We produce metal parts, components, and assemblies involving single or combination processes. Our services mainly include metal fabrication, CNC machining, casting and forging.

What are the advantages of Openex?

Openex's factory is equipped with super heavy lifting equipment capable of moving components weighing up to 800 tons which makes it easier to transport heavy-duty metal parts. Large metal parts can be shipped from our 200,000 sqm seaside workshop.

What certification does Openex have?

We are ISO, CE, ASME, API, LR certified companies. Our dedicated and professional quality control team guarantees our products meet the highest standards.

How to get an accurate pricing estimate?

We would like to have the detailed drawings and specifications of your projects, like 3D/CAD drawings, the requirement of materials and surface finish.

How quick can Openex provide a quote?

Mostly within two days, it depends on the project you inquire. To ensure the quotation accurately reflects the specifications you requested, we need to fully study on your drawings and make sure we can offer our best solution.
