Operating Since 2009

Openex Mechanical Technology Limited, based in Xiamen, China, has been in the business since 2009. We are one of the very few companies that have the capabilities to provide a full range of metalworking products and services to customers worldwide.

We produce metal parts, components, and complete assemblies involving single or combinative processes such as machining, metal fabrication, casting, forging, etc. Our products are applied to a variety of industries serving a wide range of purposes, such as Machinery, Rail Transit, Power/Energy, Boat/Bridge, Infrastructure, Oil/gas, Aerospace, etc.

We not only work with all the typical metals, such as carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum and copper alloys, but also rare metals like nickel and titanium alloys.

We have been dedicating to provide first-class quality products and services consistently to meet customers increasingly rising and specialized demands.

Services We Provide

We can handle complex metal processes like various cutting, slitting, blanking, heavy welding, large machining and assembly for a wide range of customized industrial products with competitive prices, high standard requirements and on-time delivery.

Our metal fabrication capabilities mainly include:
Laser Cutting (Max. 6000W) / Plasma Cutting / Flame Cutting (Max. 250mm) / Waterjet Cutting, Press Braking (Max. 6000t), Plate Rolling (Max. 4250×210mm), Hydraulic Pressing (Max. 5000t), Section Bending, Robotic Welding / Surfacing Welding, etc.

We have been certified with the international standards of manufacturing industry.
We use our knowledge and expertise to devise and employ solutions that not only meet, but exceed our customers' expectations, and our wealth of industry experience allows us to offer suggestions to help customers optimize designs and find more efficient production procedures.

Utilizing Advanced Equipment

We have comprehensive CNC machines and fabrication equipment to complete your projects. For example, our Schiess VMG6 PS Double Gantry 7-axis Machining Center is able to mill, turn, bore, drill and grind on large workpieces up to 50×8×7m under 450t, and also equipped with high precision CMM and laser tracking measuring machine together with robotic tool changers to speed all machining process.

Our factory locates at the seaside, large metal parts can be shipped from our littoral workshop. Our facility is equipped with super heavy lifting equipment capable of moving components weighing up to 800 tonnes (1,765,200 lbs) that makes it easier to transport heavy-duty metal parts.

We have accumulated rich experience in metalworking. We fully coordinated with our workshop departments with multiple production lines, we believe that our teams, not individuals, are the essential unit of Openex for achieving high performances and top standards throughout manufacturing in meeting all the specifications and procedures of customer satisfaction and the projects are executed in a manner that emphasizes safety, quality, and maximum cost-effectiveness.
