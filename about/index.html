Openex Mechanical Technology Limited We provide a full range of metalworking services to customers worldwide.
Get in touch with us to find the best solutions for your project.

Openex Mechanical Technology Limited, based in Xiamen, China, has been in the business since 2009. We are one of the very few companies that have the capabilities to provide a full range of metalworking products and services to customers worldwide. We produce metal parts, components, and assemblies involving single or combinative processes such as machining, metal fabrication, casting, forging, etc. Our products are applied to a variety of industries serving a wide range of purposes, such as Machinery, Rail Transit, Power/Energy, Boat/Bridge, Infrastructure, Oil/gas, Aerospace, etc.

We have world-class metalworking equipment to provide first-class machining services. For example, our Schiess VMG6 PS Double Gantry 7-axis Machining Center is able to mill, turn, bore, drill and grind on large workpieces up to 50 X 8 X 7m under 450t, and also equipped with high precision CMM and laser tracking measuring machine together with robotic tool changers to speed all machining process.

We provide a comprehensive range of custom metal fabrication services to customers around the globe. We not only work with all the typical metals, such as carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum and copper alloys, but also rare metals like nickel and titanium alloys.

Our metal fabrication services include:
Cutting, Press braking, Plate Rolling, Hydraulic Pressing, Section Bending, Welding and so on.

Our factory locates at the seaside, large metal parts can be shipped from our littoral workshop. Our facility is equipped with super heavy lifting equipment capable of moving components weighing up to 800 Tonnes (1,765,200 lbs) that makes it easier to transport heavy-duty metal parts.

We pride ourselves in being the most professional and reliable metal fabrication services supplier due to competitive prices and comprehensive capability to meet customers increasingly rising and specialized demands.
