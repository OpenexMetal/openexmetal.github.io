Openex Flame Cutting for Steel Parts

Ryan Yu, Tina Tang

Jun 28, 2021, 4:19 PM UTC+8

Our flame cutting line equipped with a 45 meters long CNC automatic flame cutting system. The maximum cutting thickness of a single torch can reach up to 100 mm.

With 24 torches working together, our cutting line can handle the large steel plate which is up to 14 meters long and 50 mm thick on the 8 meters by 14 meters platform with a tolerance of +/-1 mm.

Thanks to the help of our staff and advanced equipment, the cutting process is 10 times more efficient than common production line, not only our productivity but the section quality of steel parts is improved significantly.

The cutting pieces are typically used in machinery, rail transit, power/energy, boat/marine, bridge and infrastructure industries, etc.
