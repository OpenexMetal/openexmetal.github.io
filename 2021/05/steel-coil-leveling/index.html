Steel Coil Precision Leveling for Sheet Metal Fabrication

Openex Metal Fabrication

May 25, 2021, 3:18 PM UTC+8

Openex high precision leveling and cut-to-length line has 3 linkage leveling processes - preliminary leveling, rough leveling, and precise leveling. With these procedures, the residual stress in steel plate has been effectively eliminated.

The advanced leveling system also help make heavy fabrication much more facilitated, faster, and accurate.
