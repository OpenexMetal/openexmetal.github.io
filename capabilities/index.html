Why Openex ?

Openex has grown to a worldwide trust worthy supplier. Large machined and heavy fabricated parts produced by full series of machines are our main specialization that excels us from other metalwork providers. We are armed with all the necessary leading tools of the industry for manufacturing and inspection.

Our experienced technicians are well-trained and customer-oriented. We offer a wide range of services cover casting, machining and fabrication. North America, Japan, Europe and the Middle East are the places where most of our clients come from. High precision, cost effective, timely delivery, improving quality is the never-ending pursuit of Openex. To be a leader in high precision metal processing is our vision and mission.
